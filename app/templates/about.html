Welcome to Lab Fridays!

Lab Friday is the Parsons Lab version of a group meeting. It consists of one of the graduate students or postdocs ordering lunch for the lab and we congregate, eat, and discuss anything from science to pop culture. After lunch, we stay around the lab working and collaborating on projects.

The duties of the person who is in charge of providing lunch includes deciding on a place to order food from, sending out the relavant emails the day before Friday to give a heads up to people, and if it's a la carte, giving people enough time to get their orders in, cleaning up lunch, and finally getting reimbursed by Aaron for the meal. When you sign up for ordering lunch, you'll get instructions on how to get reimbursed.
