In order to get reimbursed, follow the instructions below.

  1. Fill out Entertainment Personal Reimbursement Claim Form
    • download and print out reimubrsment form here
    • fill out Payee information
    • enter total cost of meal and cost per attendee
    • sign and date the form (and get Aaron to sign it too)
  2. Scan the form and your receipt into one PDF document
  3. Upload onto BearBuy
    • sign in here: bearbuy.is.berkeley.edu
    • click the shopping cart icon on the left -> Go To -> View Forms
    • campus shared services -> View Form
    • fill out your name and email (leave the dept info blank)
    • activity type = entertainment reimbursement
    • fill out brief description under 'expense information' but leave expenses as $1.00!
    • attach your PDF document ("internal attachments")
    • click GO at the top
    • click "view cart details"
    • give the cart a name so you can find it later if you want
    • enter ORGNODE and ACCOUNTING CODES (get them from Aaron)
    • click SUBMIT at the top
